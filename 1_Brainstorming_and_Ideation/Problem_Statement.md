# Problem Statement

## Background

Electric Vehicles (EVs) are rapidly transforming the transportation industry due to their environmental benefits and lower operating costs. Governments, manufacturers, and consumers are increasingly investing in electric mobility, resulting in the generation of large amounts of data related to battery specifications, charging stations, driving range, energy efficiency, and vehicle performance.

Although this information is valuable, it is often stored in spreadsheets or databases that are difficult to analyze manually. Traditional reports provide limited insights and do not allow users to interact with the data effectively.

---

## Existing Challenges

The current methods of EV data analysis suffer from several limitations:

- Large datasets are difficult to interpret manually.
- Static reports provide limited insights.
- Comparing multiple EV models is time-consuming.
- Charging infrastructure information is scattered across different sources.
- Users cannot easily analyze relationships between battery capacity, driving range, charging time, and price.

---

## Need for the Project

There is a growing need for an interactive visualization platform that can transform raw electric vehicle data into meaningful graphical insights.

The proposed system should allow users to:

- Compare electric vehicle models
- Analyze charging infrastructure
- Evaluate battery performance
- Study driving range
- Compare pricing
- Identify efficient EV brands

---

## Proposed Solution

This project develops a Visualization Tool for Electric Vehicle Charge and Range Analysis using MySQL, Tableau, and Flask.

The system stores EV data in a MySQL database, performs data preparation and analysis, creates interactive Tableau dashboards and stories, and publishes them through a responsive Flask web application.

---

## Benefits

The proposed solution offers:

- Interactive dashboards
- Dynamic filtering
- Easy comparison of EV models
- Better data interpretation
- Faster decision-making
- Web-based accessibility
- User-friendly interface

---

## Conclusion

The Visualization Tool for Electric Vehicle Charge and Range Analysis addresses the limitations of traditional EV data analysis by providing an interactive Business Intelligence solution that simplifies complex datasets and enables informed decision-making.
