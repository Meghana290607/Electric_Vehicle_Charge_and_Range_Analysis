# Literature Survey

## Introduction

Electric Vehicles (EVs) have emerged as one of the fastest-growing technologies in the automobile industry due to increasing environmental concerns and government initiatives promoting sustainable transportation.

Many organizations collect extensive data regarding battery performance, charging infrastructure, driving range, pricing, and vehicle specifications. Proper visualization of this information helps users understand trends and make informed decisions.

---

# Existing Systems

Current systems include:

- Manufacturer websites
- Static reports
- Excel spreadsheets
- Basic comparison websites

Although these systems provide information, they have several limitations.

---

# Limitations of Existing Systems

- Limited visualization
- Static reports
- Difficult comparison between brands
- No centralized dashboard
- Lack of interactive filters
- Time-consuming analysis

---

# Research Findings

Studies indicate that:

- Battery capacity significantly affects driving range.
- Charging infrastructure influences EV adoption.
- Interactive dashboards improve understanding.
- Tableau enhances Business Intelligence reporting.
- Visual analytics improves decision-making.

---

# Identified Research Gap

Most existing solutions do not provide:

- Interactive dashboards
- Integrated charging infrastructure analysis
- Vehicle comparison platform
- Responsive web-based visualization
- Interactive storytelling

---

# Proposed Solution

The proposed system integrates:

- MySQL Database
- Tableau Dashboards
- Tableau Stories
- Flask Web Application

This solution provides interactive visualizations that simplify EV analysis.

---

# Advantages over Existing Systems

- Interactive dashboards
- Dynamic filtering
- Better comparison
- Improved visualization
- User-friendly interface
- Responsive web application

---

# Conclusion

The literature survey shows that Business Intelligence tools such as Tableau significantly improve the analysis of electric vehicle datasets and provide meaningful insights through interactive visualizations.
